INDIAN
The Indian flag, known as the Tiranga, features three horizontal stripes: saffron at the top symbolizing courage and sacrifice, white in the middle representing peace and truth, and green at the bottom denoting faith and chivalry. In the center, the Ashoka Chakra, a 24-spoke wheel, signifies the eternal wheel of law and dharma. This vibrant flag embodies the spirit and diversity of India, reflecting its rich history and cultural heritage.
